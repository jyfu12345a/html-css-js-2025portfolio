    Hi, I’m Jack! I was born in the Fiji Islands and later moved to Canada, where I first discovered my passion for information and web design back in high school. That spark led me to pursue Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, focusing on UX/UI design.



    Outside of design, I love making new friends and staying active through basketball, badminton, and hiking. I’m also an avid traveler, always inspired by the cultures and landscapes I encounter around the world. In my downtime, you can find me gaming on my desktop, catching up with friends and family, or trying out new food spots in the city!
